# Mnemonic Writing Guide

Guidelines for writing mnemonics for GRE vocabulary in this app.

## Direct over narrative

**Lead with the anchor and stop.** A short pointer вАФ "X contains Y", "X sounds like Y", "X = prefix + Y" вАФ sticks better than a multi-sentence scene. Skip "picture a..." / "imagine X happens" setups. 1вАУ2 sentences is usually plenty.

- Mixed English/Chinese (often the most natural)

The mnemonic needs a sound/spelling hook tied to the meaning. A vivid image alone is not enough вАФ without something tying the word's sound or shape to the image, the word won't be retrievable.

## Patterns to AVOID

### 1. Obscure roots
Breaking words into Latin/Greek roots that the average learner doesn't know.

**Bad:** `plod(heavy walk)+ding`, `dicate(rule)+or`, `sciss(cut)+ion`

**OK:** Common prefixes everyone knows: `un-`, `re-`, `anti-`, `dis-`, `inter-`, `over-`, `pre-`, `mis-`, etc.

### 2. Circular definitions (the most common failure)
The mnemonic just restates the definition вАФ no memory hook at all.

**Bad:**
- `incessant` вЖТ "something that doesn't stop is incessant" (just the definition)
- `dally` вЖТ "to dally = to dawdle, waste time" (one synonym for another)
- `dandy` вЖТ "a dandy is a man who dresses elegantly" (just the definition)
- `condemn` вЖТ "to condemn = to officially declare wrong" (just the definition)
- `confine` вЖТ "to confine = to keep within limits" (just the definition)

**Good (each ties a sound/spelling hook to the meaning):**
- `dally` вЖТ "DILLY-DALLY" (idiom contains the word)
- `condemn` вЖТ "contains DAMN вАФ to con-DEMN = to DAMN officially"
- `confine` вЖТ "shares FIN- with FINITE / FINISH вАФ set the end-bounds"

**Self-check rule:** before writing a mnemonic, point to the *exact* sound, letter group, or contained word that makes it stick. If you can't, the mnemonic is circular вАФ keep brainstorming or leave the entry empty.

### 4. Truncated / incomplete
Mnemonic cuts off mid-sentence or omits the actual meaning hook.

### 5. Forced phonetic transliteration

**Bad:** `harangue` вЖТ "еУИжЬЧеЕЛ"; `aberrant` вЖТ "еХКеИЂзДґзЙє".

## Roots/words to avoid (most learners don't know these)

- `antho-` (flower, Greek) вАФ use an anchor for *anthology* instead (e.g. "Anthony's-logy")
- `feeble` вАФ don't use as an anchor for *foible*
- `immaculate` вАФ don't use as an anchor for *maculate* (and vice versa)

`verde` is OK to use вАФ it's a common restaurant-menu word ("salsa verde"), not an obscure root.
